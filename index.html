The Proposal

Micah asked Lauren to marry him at their favorite spot in the Ward Pound Ridge Reservation. After the picnic, Micah set up the camera and asked Lauren to take a few innocent pictures. Clearly, Lauren was quite surprised when he got down on one knee.

The Wedding

Micah and Lauren will be married during the day on Saturday, June 4th 2016. The ceremony will be held at Blue Hill at Stone Barns at 11:30 followed by a luncheon reception.
