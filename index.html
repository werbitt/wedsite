The Proposal

Micah asked Lauren to marry him on a beautiful day at their favorite spot in the Meadows of Ward Pound Ridge Reservation. After a picnic, Micah set up the camera and asked Lauren to take a few innocent pictures. Clearly, Lauren was quite surprised when he got down on one knee.

The Wedding

Micah and Lauren will be married during the day on Saturday, June 4th 2016. The ceremony will be held at Blue Hill at Stone Barns at 11:30am. To be followed by a cocktail hour and luncheon reception at the same location.

Blue Hill at Stone Barns is a working Farm and all guests are welcome to walk around and say hi to the cows.

Dress code: Dressy Casual

Accommodations

Since we are having a daytime wedding, we don't have any official accommodations. But if you're coming in from out of town, here are some options.

New York City

If you stay in NYC, it takes about 35 minutes to take the train from Grand Central to Tarrytown on the Hudson Line. There will be a shuttle between the train station and Blue Hill, if you are interested please let us know here so we can get a headcount.

Tarrytown

Blue Hill at Stone Barns is located in Tarrytown, so these hotels are closest to venue.

Castle Hotel & Spa
Comfort Inn & Suites
Ridgefield

Ridgefield is a 40 minute drive from Blue Hill. You can stay here if you want a real Werbitt experience.
